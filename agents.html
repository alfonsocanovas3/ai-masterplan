Proactive lead quality optimization engine. Tests 2FA on a portion of traffic, adjusts PPL pricing per campaign type, adds/removes lead filters, proposes ad creative changes. Operates on a test-then-scale logic.

16. LP-ABTest New Cowork + Neural Box
Tests LP variants systematically: headline alternatives, hero image, CTA placement, social proof blocks, form position, page length. Sets up split traffic, monitors CVR per variant, calls statistical significance.
👤 Augments: PMMs + TMs📍 Tool: studio.dolead.com
Proven Process: Pillar 3 - Test & Learn
17. Form-ABTest New Neural Box
Optimizes the lead capture form itself. Tests: number of steps, question order, answer options, conditional logic, progress bar, CTA wording. Measures micro-conversions at each step. Critical CVR lever: form step changes can swing CVR 15-25%.
👤 Augments: PMMs + TechOps📍 Metrics: Step-by-step drop-off, overall CVR, billable rate impact
Proven Process: Pillar 4 - Scale & Run

👤 Augments: PMMs + TMs + Studio📍 Coordinates: LP-ABTest, Form-ABTest, TrafficOptimizers
Proven Process: Pillar 3 - Test & Learn
