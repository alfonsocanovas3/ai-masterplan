Implementation Roadmap

3-phase plan from foundation to fully autonomous AI operations.

🏁 Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)

March - April 2026

Get Magnus running + port existing agents to Neural Box. Focus on what already works and making it accessible to everyone via Slack.

Magnus (router) Morning-Brief (generalize) TechOps Expert (port) DealDesk (already there) CallPrep (already there) Forecast (already there) ClickUp-Ops (foundation) TechOps-Tickets (Slack-native)

Milestone: Every Doleader can type @dolead-ai in Slack and get routed to an agent. 8 agents live.

🔨 Phase 2: Core Agents (Weeks 5-12)

April - June 2026

Build the high-impact agents that save the most time. Prioritize by hours saved per week across the team.

BR-Prep (3-5h/BR saved) Campaign-Watch (real-time alerts) NRR-Optimizer (revenue impact) Quality-Monitor (daily digest) LDR-Plug (automation) LDR-Troubleshooter (proactive) LDR-Monitor (CSM daily) Market-Intel (pre-launch) Launch-Pilot (process) LP-Builder (expand Studio Expert) WebService-Plug (autonomize) Tech-Ticket (cross-functional)

Milestone: 20 agents live. Every PMM, CSM, and TechOps member uses AI daily. Estimated 40+ hours/week saved across the team.

🚀 Phase 3: Advanced & Autonomous (Weeks 13-20)

June - August 2026

Build the hardest agents: the ones that need ad platform APIs, predictive models, or multi-step autonomous workflows.

TrafficOptimizer-Search TrafficOptimizer-Social Quality-Optimizer LP-ABTest Form-ABTest ABTest-Orchestrator Dashboard-Builder Outbound-Prospector Contract-Checker

Milestone: All 32 agents live. Fully autonomous optimization loop (Campaign-Watch detects, NRR-Optimizer diagnoses, Quality-Optimizer executes). Dolead operates with AI-augmented workflows across every role.
